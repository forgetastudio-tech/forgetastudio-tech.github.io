Concept

WithBonnie is an open-world game that takes players on a journey with Bonnie through a fantasy world with diverse locations and themes, where players can deliver items to people in various towns, allowing them to explore and get to know the characters in each town.

Gameplay

Packing items for delivery

Explore beautiful locations

Deliver items to characters

Earn rewards and progress
